Gesture Volume Controller

Control your system volume using hand gestures.

Download for Windows

Features

✋ Real-time hand tracking
🎵 Control system volume with gestures
⚡ Lightweight and fast

How It Works

The app uses your webcam to detect hand movements and adjust your system volume based on the distance between your fingers.
